
Week 1: Boolean Logic
-----
Commutative Law:
---
x AND y = y AND x
x OR y = y OR x

Associative Law:
---
x AND (y AND z) = (x AND y) AND z
x OR (y OR z) = (x OR y) OR z

Distributive Law:
---
x AND (y OR z) = (x AND y) OR (x AND z)
x OR (y AND z) = (x OR y) AND (x OR z)


De Morgan Law:
---
NOT (x OR y) = NOT (x) AND NOT (y)
NOT (x AND y) = NOT (x) OR NOT (y)

Idempotent Law:
---
w AND w = w
w OR w = w
